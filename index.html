New Apartments within Walking Distance to the Sea in Kuşadası

Kuşadası, a district of Aydın, is known for its peaceful and serene atmosphere. Located near the ancient city of Ephesus, which has a history spanning thousands of years, Kuşadası is a renowned port town famous for its vibrant tourism. With its sandy beaches, ancient cities, and natural beauty, it is a frequent stop for cruise ships and remains a sought-after destination throughout history and today.

The apartments for sale in Kuşadası, Turkey are within walking distance of daily amenities. The market is 30 m away, the sea and Kadınlar Plajı (Ladies Beach) are 450 m away, the marina is 4.5 km, the shopping mall is 6.5 km, the ancient city of Ephesus is 20 km, and Adnan Menderes Airport is 63 km from the apartment.

Built on a 2,094 m² plot, the five-story new building features parking, valet service, and an elevator. Every detail has been thoughtfully designed with the buyer's comfort in mind. The building includes mid-floor 2-bedroom apartments, constructed to be earthquake-resistant.

The apartments consist of a balcony, open-plan kitchen, living room, and two bedrooms. They are equipped with laminate and ceramic flooring, air conditioning, a video intercom system, a steel entrance door, a shower cabin, and PVC windows.
